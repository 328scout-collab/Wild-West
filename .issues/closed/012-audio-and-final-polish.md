# 012: Audio and final polish

**Type:** Feature
**Priority:** Low
**Effort:** Medium
**Status:** Open

## TL;DR
Add western-jazz audio, final vector art polish, and quality-of-life improvements to complete the Aquatic Mafia experience.

## Why
Audio and polish turn the core gameplay into a finished-feeling product and improve player immersion.

## Current State
The game currently lacks music, sound effects, and final visual polish.

## Expected Outcome
Players hear western-inspired music with jazz elements, sound effects for attacks and abilities, and see refined vector UI and animation polish.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Given gameplay is running, when the player starts a round, then background music plays.
- Given actions occur, when units attack or enemies die, then appropriate sound effects play.
- Given UI screens appear, when menus and HUD elements render, then they use polished vector art or placeholder art that matches the Wild West aquatic theme.
- Given the game is tested, when the player navigates the app, then transitions and feedback feel smooth.

## Constraints
- Depends on #011.

## Progress
Created during planning session for Aquatic Mafia. Related issues: #001-#012.
